About Us

Rangmanch Drama Club is dedicated to the celebration and preservation of India's rich dramatic traditions, while exploring contemporary theater forms. Our name "Rangmanch" comes from the Hindi word for "stage" or "theater," representing our commitment to the art of storytelling through performance.

We explore various theatrical styles including traditional Indian forms like Nautanki, Jatra, and Kathakali, as well as modern plays and experimental theater. Our members develop skills in acting, script writing, direction, set design, costume creation, and technical production.

Throughout the year, we organize workshops with professional theater artists, conduct acting exercises, and produce several performances. Our major annual production showcases the collective talents of our members and draws audiences from across campus and the local community.

Join us to discover your creative potential, build confidence, and be part of a supportive artistic community that brings stories to life on stage!
