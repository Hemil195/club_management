    About

    The Robotics Club is a dynamic community of tech enthusiasts, engineers, and creative minds working together to push the boundaries of robotics and automation. We provide a hands-on learning environment where members can develop practical skills in mechanical design, electronic circuits, programming, and artificial intelligence.

    Our club welcomes students from all backgrounds and skill levels. Whether you're a beginner curious about robotics or an experienced engineer looking to challenge yourself, you'll find opportunities to learn and grow. Through collaborative projects, competitions, and workshops, members gain valuable experience that complements their academic studies and prepares them for careers in technology and engineering.

    Technologies We Work With

    Arduino

    Create interactive electronic projects with this open-source platform featuring easy-to-use hardware and software.

    Raspberry Pi

    Develop powerful robotics applications using these small single-board computers with GPIO pins for hardware control.

    ROS

    Build complex robotic systems with Robot Operating System, a flexible framework for writing robot software.

    Machine Learning

    Implement AI algorithms to create intelligent robots that can learn from data and make decisions.

    Competitions We Participate In

    High-profile international competition requiring teams to build large-scale robots to complete specific challenges.

    VEX Robotics Competition

    Year-round

    Worldwide competition with over 16,000 teams from 40+ countries designing robots to play against opponents.

    Micromouse Competition

    November

    Challenge to build small robot mice that can solve a maze in the shortest possible time.

    RoboSub

    July-August

    Competition for designing and building autonomous underwater vehicles that navigate through obstacles.
